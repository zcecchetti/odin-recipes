Crispy Baked Chicken Thighs

Description:

This is a great recipe for chicken thighs that was gievn to me by my mom. Chicken thighs stay moist, even when overcooked, as compared to chicken breasts, so for this reason I prefer to use thighs. When done right, the skin is so crispy and delicious that myself or my partner just can't get enough!

Ingredients:

  • 6 - 8 chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
