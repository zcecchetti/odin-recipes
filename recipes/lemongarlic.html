Lemon and Garlic Aioli

Description:

This is a delicious recipe that goes well on almost anything! I love dipping figasa into this. The flavor is so bright and citrusy, but the garlic and herbs really round it out well. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cups mayo
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped tarragon
  • 1 tbsp chopped rosemary
  • 1 tbsp chopped oregano
  • Juice and zest from 1 small lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Whisk together the mayo, garlic, herbs, lemon juice and zest. Season mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
